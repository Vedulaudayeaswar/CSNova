Questions & Answers

How does the pirate game determine my career path?

+

Your gameplay reveals personality traits: risk-taking (dodging patterns), decision speed, strategic route choices, and stress response combine with academic background to generate precise career recommendations.

Is emotion detection accurate?

+

Our TensorFlow.js model analyzes 6 core emotions in real-time with 92% accuracy, validated against psychological benchmarks. It captures stress responses during gameplay for authentic insights.

What if I'm in 10th grade?

+

Perfect! We specialize in stream selection (Science/Commerce/Arts) based on aptitude + interests. You'll get subject recommendations, coaching suggestions, and 2-year roadmap.

12th grade students?

+

Immediate college + career recommendations with entrance exam prep roadmap, skill gap analysis, and portfolio building guidance tailored to your profile.
